The board is numbered as follows:
-------------
| 0 | 1 | 2 |
| 3 | 4 | 5 |
| 6 | 7 | 8 |
-------------

Vectrex and Player pawn positions are kept in memory at
Play_Board_Status[0-8] 
Follows the numbering of the board.
00 = Space
01 = Player
02 = Vectrex
